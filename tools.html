What are AI Coding Agents?

AI coding agents are advanced software systems using generative AI to autonomously pursue goals and execute complex tasks. They go beyond simple automation by reasoning, planning, and learning from interactions. This guide will help you understand their profound impact on the software development lifecycle (SDLC).
